Est. 2001 · Mumbai

The Story

Age: 26 5'2" Mumbai

Esani Dey is India's youngest female guitarist, songwriter, composer, and social media influencer — featured by Rolling Stone as the youngest influencing guitarist in India.

Daughter of the legendary late Sujay Dey (bass guitarist), Esani started learning guitar at age 3 and began playing professionally for gigs and films by age 7.

She is currently Mika Singh's solo guitarist, performing at major concerts including The Big Fat Ambani Wedding — hailed as the "wedding of the decade."
