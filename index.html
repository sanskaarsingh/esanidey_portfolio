01 / About
Est. 2001 · Mumbai

The Story

Age: 26 5'2" Mumbai

Esani Dey is India's youngest female guitarist, songwriter, composer, and social media influencer — featured by Rolling Stone as the youngest influencing guitarist in India.

Daughter of the legendary late bass guitarist Sujay Dey, she started learning guitar at age 3 and began playing professionally for gigs and films by age 7.

During the quarantine, she released metal-jazz singles like Mistake (with Joel Stumbaugh, Tosief Shaikh, Sujay Dey) and Jam5 (with Darshan Doshi, Abhishek Khandelwal, Sujay Dey). Her Metal Christmas Rock Guitar version was featured heavily on Fever 104 FM.
